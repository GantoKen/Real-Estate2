Welcome to Ganto Realtors

Welcome to the Ganto Realtors website; your gateway into Kenya's real property market. We offer a wide range of services ranging from Property Valuation to Management to Estate Agency. Browse through our services section to see how Landmark can help you. We strive to render total and comprehensive professional services to our clients with competence, efficiency and due diligence. You can learn more about us by viewing the different sections under About Us menu. You can see our approach, meet the people who make Landmark tick and more. Ganto Realtors Ltd is a limited liability company founded in 1994. We are a professional firm of Registered Valuers, Management and Real Estate Consultants with our registered office in Nairobi, Kenya. Ganto Realtors Ltd works closely with each client to understand the core business needs then customizes its facility management services to provide the best solutions that meet the client's business goals.

In the process, we deliver savings to the client by:

  • Applying effective cost management
  • Putting in place effective procurement process
  • Outsourcing some of the services on behalf of the client
  • Creating optimal work environment that allows maximum output.
